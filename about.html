The Team

Here at Web Awards Awards, we are a small team of eighty members, and every one of us has been critical to the success of the company. Of course, we are filing for bankrupcy now, but that is easily explained with a expression we like to remember - "don't expand too quickly". It isn't really an expression, but we call it that to boost our morale when we are feeling depressed about the closure.

Our Mission

Since 2019, we have strived to give the best awards to those who already had them. We aim to achieve this through three very difficult and time comsuming steps, which we had previously decided not to disclose. However, since we are closing soon, the manager has graciously allowed us to share the secret formula, as long as no-one tries to claim it as their own. What you see below is a well kept trade secret that could bring you millions of dollars in revenue if you abuse it.

  1. Visit webawards.com.au
  2. Choose a site among the winners
  3. Visit the site you have chosen
  4. Analyze it and post your analysis online

We hope you guard our secret well.
